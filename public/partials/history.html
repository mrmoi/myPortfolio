MadBar FC

Amateur Soccer Team

MadBar History

Formed in 2014 by a group of old boys of Wyggeston School as "MadBar Fosse", the club joined the Small Goal Soccer Association in 2105. Before moving to Filbert Street in 2016, the club played at five different grounds, including Victoria Park south-east of the city centre and the Belgrave Road Cricket and Bicycle Grounds. The club also joined the Midland League in 2015, and was elected to Division Two of the Football League in 2015 after finishing second.
